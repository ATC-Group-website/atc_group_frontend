System Inspection

Overview

The System Inspection Department has been established to meet the latest evolving technological needs of the Local Tax Authority. This department offers two primary services:

  • Auditing of Applied Internal Financial Control Systems: Here our team conducts comprehensive audits, assessing the degree of compliance of your Internal Control Systems with Tax Authority standards.
  • Revision of System Outputs: From a tax perspective, we meticulously review system outputs, ensuring functionality and alignment with Tax Authority standards and requirements.
