Social Insurance

Overview

Our Social Insurance Department is dedicated to managing all facets of social insurance applications and effectively resolving any associated problems or disputes. We specialize in two main categories of social insurance: typical and contracting. In disputes that may arise concerning the social insurance authority, our team vigorously advocates for our clients' interests.

On behalf of our valued clients and with meticulous attention to detail and a deep understanding of the regulatory landscape, our team handles all aspects of social insurance applications and skillfully navigates any problems or disputes, whether related to typical or contracting social insurance.
